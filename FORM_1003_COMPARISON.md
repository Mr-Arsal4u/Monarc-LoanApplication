# Form 1003 Comparison Report

## Summary
This document tracks the comparison between the standard Uniform Residential Loan Application (Form 1003) and our application forms.

## Section 1: Borrower Information

### Section 1a: Personal Information ✅ COMPLETE
**Status**: Updated to match Form 1003

**Fields Added/Updated**:
- ✅ Name: First, Middle, Last, Suffix (was missing Middle/Suffix)
- ✅ SSN/ITIN: Both options now available
- ✅ Alternate Names field added
- ✅ Type of Credit (Individual/Joint) added
- ✅ Total Number of Borrowers (for joint credit)
- ✅ Other Borrower Names (for joint credit)
- ✅ Marital Status: Updated options to match Form 1003 (Married, Separated, Unmarried)
- ✅ Dependents: Added ages field (was only number)
- ✅ Contact Information: Separated into Home Phone, Cell Phone, Work Phone (with Ext.)
- ✅ Current Address: Complete address fields with Unit #, City, State, ZIP, Country
- ✅ How Long at Current Address: Years and Months
- ✅ Former Address: Conditional field (if at current address < 2 years)
- ✅ Mailing Address: Conditional field (if different from current)
- ✅ Citizenship: U.S. Citizen, Permanent Resident Alien, Non-Permanent Resident Alien
